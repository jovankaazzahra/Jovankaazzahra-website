Apple

Influence without control, the power of storytelling. The biggest thing I’ve learned at Apple is how impactful telling a good story can be. The right presentation can sell our designs, get the right stakeholders on our side, and create a better experience for our customers.

Over the past four years, I worked as a senior product designer within Apple Services. The first two years, I worked on Ad Platforms. Currently, I work on Apple Business Connect. While I can’t share much of what I worked on, my work has been shipped to millions of users and creates billions of dollars in revenue every year.

DRI (Directly Responsible Individuals)

At Apple, we have DRIs who are the driving individuals for different projects. DRIs help create ownership and accountability. I’ve been the design DRI to numerous tentpole projects that shaped two products.
